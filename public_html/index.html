Volunteering

Conservation Volunteer

I am a conservation volunteer with Ceredigion Museum. As part of a small team, we are excavating and making safe and accessible the Tudor ruins and carrying out maintenance of the thatched cottage at Y Neuadd in Llanon, Ceredigion.

We are

  • Clearing vegetation and debris from the site.
  • Pointing the stonework with lime and only re-building from photographic evidence or where safety requires it.
  • Capping the walls with lime to preserve the remaining sections.
  • Attending courses to learn more about lime and thatching.
  • Maintaining the cottage to reduce damp.
  • Flattening the ground and introducing interpretation boards for visitors.
